Boutique Memory CareOur compassion sets us apart

Our staff is specially trained to provide compassionate care and they continue to receive on-going training to ensure our high standards of care are met. Our various services include:

  • 24 hour care and supervision with night staff on duty
  • 3 nutritious and balance meals plus snacks daily
  • Total incontinence care of both bladder and bowel
  • Skin care for incontinent residents
  • Medication assistance and management
  • Doctor and medical appointments follow up
  • Provision of meaningful activities such as morning walks, exercise, gardening, etc
  • Laundry
  • General housekeeping
  • Memory Care
  • Hospice care for end of life care
  • Respite care for short term care
  • Other special needs services

Our goal has always been very simple: to bring back as much quality to the lives of the elderly whom we serve. With many years in operation, we take pride and joy in knowing that we are able to serve and serve well.

Hilltop Country Estate is a private 10 bed residential care facility for the elderly, licensed by the State of California. We are fully committed to serving the elderly who are frail with physical limitations and also those who may be suffering from mild to moderate dementia. We provide 24 hour personal care and close supervision in a private and serene homelike setting. We provide assistance and services to ambulatory and non-ambulatory resident including those needing wheelchair accessibility. For a more detailed look at our services, please click here.
